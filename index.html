About Us

Welcome to Unavagam

A Tamil restaurant offers authentic South Indian cuisine, known for its bold spices and aromatic dishes. The menu features classics like dosa, sambar, biryani, and flavorful curries, made with fresh ingredients and traditional spices. With both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, the restaurant delivers a true taste of Tamil Nadu. The dining experience often includes traditional touches, such as serving food on a banana leaf, creating a warm, vibrant atmosphere.
